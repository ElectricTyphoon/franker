This extension is for persons who study one or more languages and want to expand their vocabulary.
Franker helps you learn new words and phrases in context, enabling sentence-to-sentence translation for web sites.
You simply read news and study language, isn't that nice?

This is how it works.
You select a block of text on a web page and Franker then splits it to sentences, translates them one-by-one and injects the translation directly after every original sentence. So you read chunk of original text first, then translation. This way you learn new words quickly and in context. Moreover, you must not try to remember new words, this is not necessary, you'll meet them again soon. Just proceed reading some interesting blog post, news or article on the Web!
...or you can just use Franker as a quick translation tool.

Check also:

  • Franker for Safari
  • Franker for iPhone (and other iDevices)

Translation is powered by Google Translate service. This machine translation is good enough (to English at least) to help you understand the meaning, improve your knowledge and expand vocabulary.

Inspired by Ilya Frank's Reading Method for books.

Good luck and enjoy reading web sites in foreign languages!
